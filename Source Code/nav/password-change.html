How to Reset Your Wi-Fi Password

Step 1: Access Your Router

Open a web browser and enter your router's IP address. Common addresses: 192.168.1.1.

Enter your username and password. These are usually found on a sticker on the back of your router or in the routerâ€™s manual.

Step 3: Navigate to Network

Once logged in, find the "2.4 G / 5G" or "Wi-Fi" settings section in the menu. This may vary depending on your router model.

Step 4: Change Your Wi-Fi Password

In the Wi-Fi settings, look for the option "WLAN Security" to change your password.Enter your new password and save the changes.

Step 5: Restart Your Router (Optional)

After saving your changes, restart your router to apply the new password settings.

Note: Your new password will be required to connect to your Wi-Fi network again.
